## How to Use This Kit

### Step 1 — Know What You Sell
Read `01_Services_Menu.md` first. Pick 1–2 services to lead with (recommendation: AI Receptionist + Automation Workflows). Don't try to offer everything on day one.

### Step 2 — Set Up Your Legal Foundation
Customize `02_Client_Contract_Template.md` with your business name, address, and preferred payment terms. Run it by a lawyer if you're doing deals over $2,000.

### Step 3 — Start Outreach
Copy a script from `03_Cold_Outreach_Scripts.md` that matches your target industry. Personalize the first line. Send 10–20 a day.

### Step 4 — Run Great Discovery Calls
Use `04_Discovery_Call_Script.md` before every call. The first few times, literally read from it. After a few calls, it'll be second nature.

### Step 5 — Price Without Apology
Read `05_Pricing_Guide.md` once before your first call. Understand value-based pricing. Never quote hourly.

### Step 6 — Deliver Professionally
Use `06_Onboarding_Checklist.md` the moment a client signs. It signals competence immediately.

### Step 7 — Build Social Proof
After your first result, fill in `07_Case_Study_Template.md`. Post it everywhere.

### Step 8 — Execute the Launch Plan
Follow `08_30_Day_Launch_Plan.md` day by day. Don't skip steps. Trust the process.

## A Word on Mindset

You don't need to be a developer. You don't need an office, a team, or a website. You need:
- A clear offer
- A way to reach people
- Something that works

This kit gives you all three starting points.

The gap between someone who makes $0 and someone who makes $10K/month with an AI agency is almost never skill. It's consistent outreach + one good close.

Go close something.
