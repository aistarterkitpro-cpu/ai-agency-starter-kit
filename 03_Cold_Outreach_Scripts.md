# Cold Outreach Scripts
### 5 Proven DM/Email Scripts for AI Agency Prospecting

---

**Before You Send Anything — Read This:**

The biggest mistake people make with cold outreach is leading with features instead of pain. Nobody cares about "cutting-edge AI solutions." They care about not missing calls, not wasting staff time, and not losing leads to competitors.

These scripts are built around that principle. Each one:
- Opens with something specific (not generic)
- Names a real problem they're likely experiencing
- Makes a small, low-risk ask
- Never tries to close in the first message

**Personalization rule:** Change the first line for every single prospect. That's where 80% of your reply rate comes from. The rest of the script can stay templated.

## Script 1: Restaurant / Food & Beverage

**Best channel:** Instagram DM, Facebook DM, or email
**Best timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10am–2pm

---

### Version A — DM (Short)

> Hey [Name] — I follow [Restaurant Name] and your [mention something specific: new menu, recent post, location] caught my eye.
>
> Quick question: does your team spend a lot of time answering the same phone calls — hours, reservations, menu questions?
>
> I help restaurants set up an AI system that handles those calls 24/7 so staff can focus on guests who are actually there. Took one of my clients from missing 40% of after-hours calls to zero.
>
> Would it be weird if I showed you what that looks like for a place like yours? (Takes like 15 min — no pitch deck, I promise.)

---

### Version B — Email

**Subject:** Quick question about [Restaurant Name]'s front-of-house

> Hi [Name],
>
> I came across [Restaurant Name] while looking at [neighborhood / cuisine type] spots in [City] — [specific compliment about their Google reviews / menu / vibe].
>
> I work with restaurants to set up AI systems that handle reservations, answer menu questions, and capture leads after hours — basically a virtual front-desk that never takes a sick day.
>
> One restaurant I worked with in [comparable city/type] was losing about $3,000/month in bookings from missed after-hours calls. Three weeks after we set up their AI receptionist, they'd recaptured most of that.
>
> I'm not trying to sell you anything on this email — I just want to know if this is actually a problem for you. Is it?
>
> If yes, I'd love to spend 15 minutes showing you what we built for them.
>
> — [Your Name]
> [Your Agency] | [Phone/Link]

---

### Follow-Up (3–5 days later if no reply)

> Hey [Name] — just bumping this up in case it got buried. I know restaurant inboxes are chaos.
>
> Still happy to show you the system if you're curious. If it's not relevant right now, totally fine — just let me know and I'll leave you alone. 😄

## Script 2: Real Estate Agent / Brokerage

**Best channel:** LinkedIn DM or email
**Best timing:** Monday–Wednesday, 9–11am or 4–6pm

---

### Version A — LinkedIn DM

> Hi [Name] — I saw you're doing some impressive volume in [area/neighborhood] — the [specific listing or achievement you noticed on their profile] stood out.
>
> I specialize in setting up AI lead qualification systems for agents. Basically: when someone fills out a form on your site or ads, an AI immediately texts/emails them back, qualifies them (timeline, budget, pre-approval status), and books a call with you — while you're showing a house.
>
> Most agents I talk to are either not following up fast enough, or wasting time on unqualified leads. Sound familiar at all?
>
> Not pitching you anything — just curious if that's a real pain point. Happy to show you a demo if so.

---

### Version B — Email

**Subject:** Re: your leads at [Brokerage Name]

> Hi [Name],
>
> Found your profile through [LinkedIn / Zillow / your website] — you're clearly doing strong work in [market area].
>
> I build AI lead qualification and follow-up systems for real estate agents. Here's the core problem it solves: when a lead comes in at 11pm, most agents respond the next morning — and by then, that lead has already talked to two other agents.
>
> The AI system texts them back in under 60 seconds, qualifies their situation, and books a call on your calendar. You wake up to a scheduled appointment instead of a cold lead.
>
> I'm not going to claim it works for everyone — but for agents running paid ads or getting any volume of online leads, the ROI is usually fast and obvious.
>
> Can I send you a 2-minute Loom showing exactly how it works?
>
> — [Your Name]

**Best timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 11am–1pm

>

---

>
> The typical client recovers $2,000–$6,000/month in appointments that would have otherwise gone cold.
>
> Worth a 15-minute conversation? I can show you exactly what the system looks like.
>
> — [Your Name]
> [Agency] | [Website]

## Script 4: General Small Business (Catch-All)

**Best channel:** Email, LinkedIn, or cold DM
**Best use:** When you've found a local business with good reviews but obvious gaps (slow response time, no chat, missed calls mentioned in reviews)

---

### Version A — Short DM

> Hey [Name] — noticed [Business Name] while looking at [category] businesses in [City]. Really solid reviews.
>
> I do one thing: help small businesses stop losing leads to slow response times. I set up AI systems that respond instantly to inquiries — website, Google, social — 24/7.
>
> Is that something that's on your radar, or is your current lead response process working well?

---

### Version B — Email (Use When You've Done Research)

**Subject:** [Business Name] — I noticed something

> Hi [Name],
>
> I came across [Business Name] and spent a few minutes on your site. [Specific observation — e.g., "You don't have a chat widget, which probably means you're missing some leads who visit after hours" or "I noticed a few reviews mention not being able to reach you by phone"]
>
> I build AI systems for local businesses that handle lead capture and response automatically — so you're not dependent on someone manually checking inboxes or answering calls.
>
> Most business owners I talk to are surprised by how much revenue slips through the cracks from slow or missed responses. The average small business loses 20–30% of inbound leads this way.
>
> I'd love to do a quick 15-minute audit of your current lead response process — no charge, no obligation. I'll either find something worth fixing, or I'll tell you your process looks solid and wish you well.
>
> Worth a quick chat?
>
> — [Your Name]
> [Agency] | [Phone] | [Calendar Link]

## Script 5: Follow-Up Sequence

**Use when:** Prospect hasn't replied after first message (wait 4–7 days before first follow-up)

---

### Follow-Up 1 (Day 5 after initial message)

> Hey [Name] — just wanted to bump this up. I know you're busy.
>
> If AI-powered [lead capture / booking / automation] isn't relevant right now, no worries at all — just say the word and I won't reach out again.
>
> If there's any curiosity, I'm happy to send a quick 2-minute video showing exactly what we built for a [similar business type].

---

### Follow-Up 2 (Day 10 — if still no reply)

> Last bump, I promise.
>
> I put together a short case study on how [similar business] used an AI chatbot to [specific result: capture 40 more leads/month, save 8 hours/week on phone calls, etc.].
>
> Happy to send it over if you're curious — no call required. Just a quick read.
>
> Either way, hope [Business Name] is crushing it. 🙌

---

### Follow-Up 3 (Day 21 — "break-up" message)

> Hey [Name] — I've reached out a couple times and haven't heard back, so I'll take this as a "not right now" — completely understand.
>
> I'm closing out my outreach for [City / industry] this week. If AI tools for your business ever become relevant, you know where to find me.
>
> Wishing you a great [season / quarter / year].
>
> — [Your Name]

*(Note: Break-up messages sometimes get the most replies. People respond when they think the door is closing.)*

## Outreach Tracking System

Track these numbers weekly:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Messages sent per day | 15–25 |
| Reply rate | 5–15% |
| Calls booked per week | 3–7 |
| Calls that convert to paid | 20–40% |

**If reply rate is below 5%:** Your opener isn't specific enough. Add more personalization.

**If calls book but don't convert:** Your pricing or discovery call process needs work — see the Discovery Call Script.
