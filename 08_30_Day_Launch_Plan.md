# 30-Day AI Agency Launch Plan
## Zero to First Client in 30 Days

---

## Week 1 — Foundation (Days 1–7)

**Goal: Get ready to sell before you build anything**

- [ ] Day 1: Define your niche (pick ONE industry: restaurants, real estate, med spas, etc.)
- [ ] Day 2: Create your services menu (use the template in this kit)
- [ ] Day 3: Set up Gumroad or Stripe for payments
- [ ] Day 4: Create a simple one-page website or Linktree
- [ ] Day 5: Set up professional email (business@yourdomain or Gmail)
- [ ] Day 6: Identify 50 target businesses in your niche (Google Maps works fine)
- [ ] Day 7: Rest. Review everything. Make sure your offer is clear.

---

## Week 2 — Outreach (Days 8–14)

**Goal: Get 3 discovery calls booked**

- [ ] Day 8: Personalize and send 10 cold DMs/emails (use the scripts in this kit)
- [ ] Day 9: Follow up on Day 8 outreach + send 10 new
- [ ] Day 10: Post on LinkedIn/Twitter about AI for your target niche (value post, no pitch)
- [ ] Day 11: Send 10 more outreach messages
- [ ] Day 12: Join 3 Facebook Groups where your target clients hang out. Provide value.
- [ ] Day 13: Send 10 more outreach. Follow up on anyone who opened/replied.
- [ ] Day 14: Review what's working. Double down on the channel getting responses.

**Target by end of Week 2:** 3 discovery calls booked

---

## Week 3 — Close (Days 15–21)

**Goal: Close your first paid client**

- [ ] Run your discovery calls (use the script in this kit)
- [ ] Send proposals within 24 hours of each call
- [ ] Follow up 48 hours after proposal if no response
- [ ] Continue daily outreach (10/day minimum)
- [ ] Post 3 more value pieces on social media
- [ ] Offer a "Founding Client" rate to the first 3 clients (10–20% discount for testimonial)

**Target by end of Week 3:** 1 paid client, contract signed, deposit collected

---

## Week 4 — Deliver & Scale (Days 22–30)

**Goal: Deliver, get testimonial, close client #2**

- [ ] Deliver on your first client (use onboarding checklist)
- [ ] Document everything you do for replication
- [ ] Ask for early feedback (and a quote you can use)
- [ ] Continue outreach — do not stop when you get busy
- [ ] Pitch retainer to first client at 30-day mark
- [ ] Close client #2

---

## Month 2+ Growth Path

| Month | Target | How |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | $500–$1,500 | 1–3 one-time projects |
| Month 2 | $2,000–$3,500 | 2–4 projects + 1–2 retainers |
| Month 3 | $4,000–$6,000 | Mix of projects + 5–8 retainers |
| Month 6 | $8,000–$12,000 | Primarily retainers, referrals fueling growth |

---

## Non-Negotiable Daily Actions (Every Single Day)

1. Send 10 outreach messages
2. Follow up on previous outreach
3. Post one piece of content
4. Learn one new thing about AI tools

30 days of this = a real business. Skip days = stay stuck.
